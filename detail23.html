Ifs Ands or Buts

January 23, 2016 - May 3, 2016

Rockbund Art Museum

Singaporean artist Heman Chong takes over RAM for his first solo exhibition in Mainland China. The show features installation (including one piece on the outside of the building) and video as Chong explores 'spaces between text and images'.
