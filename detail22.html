Youn Myeung-Ro: Traces of the Spirit

February 21, 2016 - March 20, 2016

Alioth Art Center

Youn Myeung-Ro: Traces of the Spirit is one of the series, Retrospectives for Contemporary Masters - National Museum of Contemporary Art, Korea aimed to help art lovers have an overview of the Korean modern art and a deeper understanding of the contemporary artists who made important contributions to the history of Korean art. In this particular event, visitors are invited to the world of art Youn Myeung-Ro established via the creative abstract paintings he produced through his 50-year career which started in the late 1950s. The event will be particularly memorable to art lovers thanks to, among others, several large paintings he completed in a recent period.
